About Mars

Mars is the fourth planet in the solar system, it’s surface are made up of oxygenated iron then let the planet being red.

Martian size is half size of the earth, one-tenth the mass of the Earth, you can jump even higher than on Earth due to its low gravity.

A year in Mars lasts 687 days, one Martian day is 24hr and 40 minutes.

Mars has a thin atmosphere and lack of oxygen and nitrogen.

The average temperature is -55℃, when sun rises, its equator will reach 20℃.
